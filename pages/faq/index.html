TECHNOLOGY FESTIVAL 2024

What is TechFest 2024?

Technology Festival (TECHFEST) 2024 is a series of national scale events held by HIMTI annually. It is designed to foster creativity and competition among students across Indonesia.

What’s the theme of TECHFEST 2024?

The theme of this year’s TECHFEST is “Tech Fusion: Bridging Creativity and Technology”.

What are the activities of TECHFEST 2024?

TECHFEST 2024 consists of three main activities, namely competitions, exhibitions (TechExpo), and seminars (TechTalk).

Where will TECHFEST 2024 be held?

TECHFEST 2024 activities will be held as follows: competitions at BINUS University @Kemanggisan - Anggrek Campus, TechExpo online on this website, and TechTalk both onsite at BINUS University @Kemanggisan - Anggrek Campus and online via Zoom.

What competition will be held in TECHFEST 2024?

TECHFEST 2024 will host two competitions, namely UI/UX Design and Competitive Programming.

How do I register?

Will there be a registration fee?

Yes, the registration fees are as follows: Rp120,000 for Competitive Programming, and Rp15,000 for UI/UX Design, and for TechExpo and TechTalk will not be charged [Pls Confirm this]

Can participants register for several competitions at the same time?

Yes, participants can register for several competitions, but team leaders can only participate in one. (ini gw berdasarkan web tahun lalu)

What is the maximum number of participants in each team?

The number of participants in each team are as follows: 3 people for competitive programming and 1-3 people for UI/UX Design.

Can team members come from different universities?

Team members must come from the same university, but may be from different majors.

Are there any prerequisites for participating?

Participants of the competitions must be an active student of higher study(S1/D3/D4).

Will participants receive certificates?

Yes, each participant will receive a certificate.

How can I stay updated on TECHFEST 2024?

Stay updated by following our social media: @techfest.himti on Instagram and @techfesthimti on TikTok.
