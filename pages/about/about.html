About Us

Technology Festival 2024

BACKGROUND

TECHFEST 2023 is a competitive event organized by HIMTI at Binus University, designed to foster creativity and competition among students across Indonesia. This year's TECHFEST, themed "Tech Fusion: Bridging creativity and Technology," will take place at Binus University’s Kemanggisan - Anggrek Campus.

HISTORY

The Technology Festival (TECHFEST) was first held in Malang under the name “HIMTI Festival” in 2021 online due to the Covid-19 pandemic. However, this did not dampen the enthusiasm of the participants to be able to participate and enliven the series of HIMTI Festival 2021 activities.

GOALS

TECHFEST 2023 aims to test the skills of students nationwide through various case study competitions, promoting innovation. Attendees will benefit from seminars, build new connections, and foster collaboration among different organizations.

ABOUT LOGO

The vibrant and dynamic emblem of TECHFEST 2024: a fusion of colorful geared brush strokes. With elements that complement each other. The gear symbolizes the driving force of technology, while the brush evokes the boundless creativity that ignites progress. These elements harmonize to represent our mission: to bridge the gap between creativity and technology.
